About StarkFlow

StarkFlow is a groundbreaking, fun, and interactive Telegram-based game built on Starknet. We're designed to seamlessly introduce new users to the world of blockchain through engaging gameplay. Users gradually learn about Starknet wallets, tokens, and NFTs in a simple and accessible manner, helping onboard more users to the network while providing an enjoyable experience.

Key Features

Telegram Wallet

Trade on Starknet directly from your Telegram account, making blockchain interactions as easy as sending a message.

Gaming dApp

Earn airdrop points while playing engaging mini-games, all within the Telegram platform you already know and love.

Mining Rewards

Claim rewards every 4 hours, introducing you to the concept of yield farming in a fun, gamified way.

How It Works

  1. Start playing instantly on Telegram - no blockchain knowledge required
  2. Create a Starknet wallet as you progress through the game
  3. Earn tokens and NFTs while learning about blockchain concepts
  4. Engage with advanced features like trading NFTs and interacting with smart contracts

Powered by Starknet

StarkFlow is built on Starknet, a layer 2 scaling solution that provides fast, secure, and cost-effective transactions. By leveraging Starknet's technology, we offer a seamless gaming experience with the benefits of blockchain, without the typical barriers to entry.
